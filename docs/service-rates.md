# Service Rates and Pricing Benchmarks

This document is a planning reference, not a published fee schedule.

## Property Management

Common pricing models:

- Percentage of collected rent.
- Per-door monthly fee.
- Lease-up fee.
- Maintenance coordination fee.
- Project management fee.

Waterhouse should maintain pricing by property type, community size, reporting expectations, and owner service level.

## Manufactured Home Sales and Infill

Common pricing considerations:

- Home sale margin or commission.
- Setup coordination fee.
- Delivery coordination fee.
- Lot preparation project management.
- Removal coordination.

Pricing should reflect lot complexity, utility work, manufacturer coordination, and sales support.

## Asphalt Paving

Common pricing drivers:

- Square footage.
- Scope type.
- Surface condition.
- Material costs.
- Mobilization.
- Traffic control.
- Grading or compaction requirements.

Scope categories:

- Crack fill.
- Slurry seal.
- Grind-down.
- Partial removal.
- Full removal.
- Resurfacing.

## Brokerage

Common pricing models:

- Commission on sale.
- Consulting fee.
- Retainer for special projects.
- Referral fee where legal and appropriate.

Property types:

- Manufactured homes.
- Mobile home parks.
- Apartment properties.
- Raw land.
- Residential homes.
- Commercial property.

## Internal Recommendation

Keep public pricing limited unless the service is standardized. Use internal rate cards for managers and owner proposals.
