# Expanded Services Strategy

Waterhouse already spans several related operating lines. This document frames how those services can be expanded and packaged.

## Property and Asset Management

- Community onboarding packages.
- Owner reporting packages.
- Resident communication workflows.
- Maintenance triage and vendor coordination.
- Compliance calendar management.

## Manufactured Home Dealership and Infill

- Lot readiness assessments.
- Home replacement planning.
- Manufacturer comparison support.
- Buyer readiness packages.
- Delivery and setup coordination.

## Asphalt Paving and Site Work

- Annual pavement condition reviews.
- Community road maintenance plans.
- Parking lot resurfacing programs.
- Emergency repair estimates.
- Owner-facing capital planning summaries.

## Brokerage and Investment Support

- Manufactured home sales.
- Mobile home park transactions.
- Apartment property transactions.
- Land and investment opportunity support.
- Portfolio disposition planning.

## Portal-Enabled Differentiation

Each service line should eventually connect to a portal workflow:

- Tenants: maintenance and notices.
- Owners: reports, documents, projects.
- Managers: internal operations.
- Admins: knowledge base and system controls.
