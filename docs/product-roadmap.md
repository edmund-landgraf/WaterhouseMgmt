# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: Operating Map

Goal: turn the scrape into a usable internal planning app.

Included now:

- business-line overview
- Waterhouse pages inventory
- community portfolio list
- SaaS component cross-reference
- provider examples

Next improvements:

- add editable records
- add confidence/source notes per claim
- add service taxonomy
- add export to Markdown/CSV
- add screenshots or cached page captures

## Phase 2: CRM and Intake

Goal: normalize inbound demand across all business lines.

Build:

- contact database
- lead intake forms
- source tracking
- inquiry routing
- business-line classification
- follow-up tasks

Intake types:

- resident inquiry
- park owner management inquiry
- asphalt quote request
- manufactured home buyer lead
- home infill project lead
- brokerage buyer/seller lead
- vendor/contractor contact

## Phase 3: Community and Property Operations

Goal: model managed communities and operational workflows.

Build:

- community records
- resident records
- lot/site records
- home records
- maintenance tickets
- inspection records
- vendor assignments
- rent/payment status placeholders
- owner reporting dashboard

## Phase 4: Dealership and Infill Workflow

Goal: manage the lifecycle from vacant lot or obsolete home to occupied new home.

Build:

- lot readiness tracker
- demolition/removal work order
- lot prep project
- manufacturer quote comparison
- home selection record
- delivery schedule
- setup punch list
- buyer pipeline
- financing referral state
- sale closeout

## Phase 5: Asphalt Paving Workflow

Goal: support quoting, scheduling, execution, and closeout for paving jobs.

Build:

- quote intake
- pavement assessment
- estimate builder
- crew calendar
- equipment assignments
- material log
- job photos
- change orders
- closeout report
- invoice handoff

## Phase 6: Brokerage Workflow

Goal: track real estate deals from lead to close.

Build:

- buyer/seller pipelines
- property records
- listing records
- commercial deal pipeline
- due diligence checklist
- document room
- closing milestone tracker

## Phase 7: Integration Layer

Goal: connect best-of-breed tools without rebuilding every commodity feature.

Build:

- integration credentials vault
- import/export jobs
- webhook receiver
- provider mapping records
- audit logs
- sync status dashboard

Priority integrations:

- property management platform
- CRM
- e-signature
- payment provider
- accounting
- maps/geocoding
- listing channels

## Phase 8: Executive Reporting

Goal: show the business as a portfolio of operating engines.

Dashboards:

- occupancy and vacancies
- active maintenance load
- infill project pipeline
- home sales pipeline
- asphalt quote and job pipeline
- brokerage pipeline
- vendor performance
- cash collection / AR summary
- owner reporting status
