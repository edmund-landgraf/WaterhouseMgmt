# Lead Sources and Scouting Workflow

This guide adapts the BrokerBizOps lead-source strategy to Waterhouse's operating lines.

## Property Management Lead Sources

- Owner referrals.
- Community owner associations.
- Manufactured housing industry events.
- Local property owner networks.
- Search traffic for community and apartment management.
- Broker and lender referrals.

## Manufactured Home Sales and Infill Lead Sources

- Existing community lot inventory.
- Resident upgrade inquiries.
- Park-owned home replacement needs.
- Manufacturer relationships.
- Buyer inquiries from the public website.
- Community manager referrals.

## Asphalt Paving Lead Sources

- Existing managed communities.
- Owner capital improvement plans.
- Property managers.
- Apartment owners.
- Mobile home park owners.
- Referral from maintenance vendors.

## Brokerage Lead Sources

- Existing owner relationships.
- Manufactured housing investor networks.
- Apartment owner referrals.
- Land and investment property inquiries.
- Public contact form submissions.

## Scout and Secure Workflow

1. Log lead source.
2. Assign service line.
3. Capture property/community context.
4. Determine urgency and fit.
5. Schedule call or site review.
6. Create task list and document folder.
7. Track follow-up in the manager portal.

## Lead Quality Factors

- Clear property or community need.
- Decision-maker identified.
- Timeline stated.
- Budget or scope range known.
- Service line fit confirmed.
- Geographic fit confirmed.
