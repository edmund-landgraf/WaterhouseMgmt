# Local SEO and Google Business Profile Strategy

Local search should support Waterhouse's community, property, paving, sales, and brokerage services.

## Google Business Profile

Recommended categories:

- Property Management Company
- Real Estate Agency
- Mobile Home Dealer
- Paving Contractor

Service areas should be specific to Waterhouse's actual operating footprint.

## Review Strategy

Useful review language:

- Responsive management.
- Clear communication.
- Professional maintenance coordination.
- Helpful manufactured home sales support.
- Reliable paving project coordination.

Avoid asking for reviews that disclose resident-sensitive details.

## Local Landing Pages

- Manufactured home community management by region.
- Asphalt paving for community roads and parking areas.
- Manufactured home sales and replacement support.
- Brokerage for parks, apartments, homes, and land.

## Schema Markup

Use structured data where appropriate:

- `LocalBusiness`
- `RealEstateAgent`
- `ProfessionalService`
- `Service`

## Content Topics

- How manufactured home communities plan pavement maintenance.
- What owners should expect from community management.
- Manufactured home replacement and lot readiness basics.
- How brokerage differs for mobile home parks and apartment investments.
- Resident maintenance request best practices.
