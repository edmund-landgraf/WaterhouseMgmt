# Google Ads Strategy

This strategy adapts the BrokerBizOps advertising model to Waterhouse's services.

## Primary Keyword Verticals

### Property Management

- `manufactured home community management california`
- `mobile home park management company`
- `apartment property management roseville`
- `property management for manufactured housing`

### Manufactured Home Sales and Infill

- `manufactured home dealer california`
- `mobile home infill services`
- `manufactured home replacement for mobile home park`
- `new manufactured home setup coordination`

### Asphalt Paving

- `mobile home park asphalt paving`
- `community road paving contractor`
- `parking lot slurry seal california`
- `asphalt repair for property managers`

### Brokerage

- `mobile home park broker california`
- `manufactured home broker`
- `apartment investment property broker`
- `land brokerage california`

## Negative Keywords

- `free`
- `jobs`
- `DIY`
- `used trailer parts`
- `rv park`
- `campground`
- `tiny house plans`

## Landing Page Strategy

- Property management ads should land on `/services` or a dedicated management page.
- Infill and dealership ads should land on a manufactured home service section.
- Paving ads should land on asphalt-specific content.
- Brokerage ads should land on brokerage service content.
- Contact CTAs should route to `/contact`.

## Success Metrics

- Qualified contact submissions.
- Phone calls.
- Cost per qualified lead.
- Service-line conversion rate.
- Community or owner inquiries by geography.
